---
title: "Generate a trip"
api: "POST https://trottapi.onrender.com/IA"
description: "This endpoint generates given data for a trip itinerary"
---

### Params

<ParamField type="string" header="Authorization">
    Header in form of Bearer \<jwt\>
</ParamField>

### Body

<ParamField body="lon" type="number">
  This is the longitude of our central point, i.e the hotel.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="lat" type="number">
  This is the latitude of our central point, i.e the hotel.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="days" type="number">
  This is the number of days the trip is supposed to lengh.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="transportMean" type='"driving" | "walking" | "cycling"' required="false" default='walking'>
  The method of transport used by the user. Defaults to walking.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="interests" type='string' required="false">
  The interests of the user separated by commas.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="bbox" type='number[4]' required="false">
  The bounding box surrounding the city. If not provided the old method of radius will be used.
</ParamField>
